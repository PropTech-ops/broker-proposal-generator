## A. Dubai Office Investment Proposal

### Client Requirement

- Client name: Rohan Mehta
- Company name: Mehta Global Holdings
- Investment goal: Income-generating fitted commercial office in Dubai
- Preferred asset: Fitted office with leasing potential
- Budget: AED 3.5M to AED 4M
- Target yield: 8% to 9%
- Client Notes: Wants a ready commercial asset with rental income potential, strong accessibility, and medium-term capital appreciation.

### Property Snapshot

- Property name: Iris Bay Office Tower
- Location: Business Bay, Dubai
- Property type: Fully fitted office
- Area: 1,850 sq.ft
- Availability: Immediate
- Key highlights: Fitted office, professional tower, Business Bay location, close to Downtown Dubai and Sheikh Zayed Road.

### Investment Snapshot

- Sale Price: AED 3.65M
- Expected Monthly Rent: AED 27,500
- Expected Annual Rental Income: AED 330,000
- Expected Yield: 9.0%
- Occupancy Status: Vacant / ready to lease
- Expected Appreciation: Medium-term upside linked to Business Bay office demand
- Exit Potential: Strong investor and end-user demand for manageable fitted office sizes

### Risks

- Title/document risk: Title deed and ownership status to be verified
- Parking risk: Parking allocation to be confirmed
- CAM clarity: Service charges and owner association dues to be checked
- Other notes: Validate achievable rent using recent Business Bay office comparables

### Next Steps

- Verify Documents: Review title deed, ownership status, and building approvals
- Confirm Charges: Check service charges, parking allocation, and owner association dues
- Inspect Asset: Inspect fit-out, AC, MEP, pantry/washroom access, and condition
- Validate Rent: Compare achievable rent against recent Business Bay office leases
- Submit Offer: Proceed with offer subject to due diligence and final commercials
